Fantasy Baseball Starting Pitching Summary

I am an avid fan of baseball and have been a member of a dedicated dynasty fantasy baseball league since 2018. Through this league, my interest in advanced baseball analytics was born and I have spent a significant amount of time digging through statistics like FIP, WAR, and BABIP to identify the next great free agent pickup for my team.

This dashboard serves as a 2022 year-end summary of our teams' starting pitching performances and efficiencies. This dashboard was intended to show relative fantasy performances (focusing on fantasy points scored) so each manager could benchmark their team's performances against others and the league average. The dashboard includes summary statistics, rolling aggregates, scatter plots, and editable distributions.
